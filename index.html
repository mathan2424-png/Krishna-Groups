Project Specifications

01

The Foundation & Frame

  • Pile foundation as per Structural Drawing.
  • R.C.C Framed Structure with Columns & Beams.
  • External & Partition Walls in Cement / AAC Blocks.
  • Internal Gypsum & External Cement Plastering.
02

Bespoke Flooring

  • 2 X 2 Vitrified Tiles for Living & Dining.
  • Anti-Skid Tiles in Balcony & Restrooms.
  • Premium tiling for Bedrooms and Kitchen.
  • Architectural floor patterns for common areas.
03

Apertures & Access

  • Main Door: Teak Wood Veneer finish.
  • Internal: Engineered Door Frame - Laminate.
  • UPVC French doors with Sliding glass.
  • MS Grill for all windows as per design.
04

Gourmet Kitchen

  • Wall tiles up to 2' above Granite Platform.
  • SS sink with drain board for all kitchens.
  • Provision for Water Purifier & Dish Washer.
  • Sleek architectural plumbing fixtures.
05

The Sanctuary

  • Kohler / Jaquar or equivalent closets.
  • Premium Kohler / Jaquar CP Fittings.
  • Shower partition & False Ceiling included.
  • Provision for Geyser and Exhaust Fan.
06

Energy & Connection

  • Three Phase Power Supply with EB meters.
  • Modular Switches & Split AC provision.
  • 1.5 KVA Power Back up included.
  • TV & Data points in all main rooms.
